---
name: signal-policy-calibration
description: Turn memory and feedback into review-only improvement proposals.
---

## When to use

Use during calibration or learning observation.

## Inputs

ProjectMemory, SignalMemory, FeedbackMemory, PolicyMemory, replay results.

## Outputs

Policy, skill, and watchlist proposals requiring approval.

## Steps

1. Summarize recurring positive and negative feedback.
2. Propose minimal policy or workflow changes.
3. Compare old and proposed behavior with replay.

## Constraints

Memory is infrastructure. Never apply proposals or edit formal configuration.
